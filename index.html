Our Journey

The Penuel Story

2018 - The Beginning

Founded after spotting a gap in the Ugandan market that was dominated by imported chocolate products. After thorough research, Penuel Chocolate was born as a Ugandan chocolate brand.

Local Sourcing

We source cocoa from specific regions of Uganda to create bean to bar chocolate with unique yet relatable taste. Our products consider all age brackets and people from different walks of life.

Educating Palates

Many people in Africa have misunderstood chocolate because the market hasn't offered enough options. We make varieties from Dark, Bitter, Semi-sweet, Sweet, Milk, and chocolates with inclusions like nuts and banana chips.

Our Vision

To build a global chocolate empire that is a pillar to sustainable development through fair trade, biodiversity, job opportunities, and promoting gender-based equality in the community.
